Why green screen?

Green screen is used for anything that would require the background to be changed.

This technique is also referred to as colour keying or chroma key.

Chroma keying can be done with backgrounds of any colour that are
uniform and distinct, but green and blue backgrounds are more
commonly used because they differ most distinctly in hue from most human
skin colours.
