EMAIL

Integrated email and storage, Office 365, GSuite, local Server IMAP and more bespoke solutions as required.

It is no secret that in recent years the world of collaboration and especially email has been radically changing. Whereas previously, if an organisation desired a reliable, secure and integrated email system they would have had to invest in one or more email servers running something like Microsoft Exchange or Lotus Domino.

Thankfully some of the biggest players in the market are now offering equivalent collaboration systems to businesses via the cloud, with no need for in house technicians or any major investment at all!

Both Microsoft and Google have fully featured, scalable collaboration suites and office packages that can provide white label email solutions without any overheads – just a flat monthly fee per user.

We recommend that our clients take advantage of either Google Mail or Office 365 for their email needs. The best part is that we are here to help you make the transition.

Talk to us today about moving your organisation’s email to Office 365 or Google Mail.
