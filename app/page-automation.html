AUTOMATION & INTEGRATION

HOME / DEPARTMENTS

AUTOMATION

Automation is the key to running an efficient and productive business. We believe that your software should, at the very least, make your life easier. Beyond this you should be able to trust your software to perform all of the fiddly, repetitive and menial tasks that free you up to be awesome doing what you do best.

With properly designed automation you can exponentially increase your productivity. From email reminders through to automated billing or stock control, you can leverage accuracy and millisecond timing of computing to keep your business working even when you are not!

INTEGRATION

Everyone loves it when things work together seamlessly. In this day and age we all expect our applications to play nicely together. We want our calendar to talk to our inbox and our Instagram feed to be shared on Facebook!

Almost all web based software solutions offer some kind of application program interface or API, for communicating directly with the service from code. Many also include built-in and “no code” ways to connect different services together.

We specialise in helping you get the most out of your software, whether it is writing software to pull all of your data together into a single interface or consultancy and support in connecting everything together in the best way possible.

TECHNOLOGY

We get excited about technology, and we love doing new and innovative things with cutting edge tools!

The tools, services and technologies we use varies considerably from solution to solution. A major part of the initial consultancy process establishes what tools will meet your requirements.

At Life Solutions we have experience and expertise in a wide range of technologies and services. We can offer software development using a variety of different programming languages and frameworks.

Technology is constantly moving forwards, and as such we strive to stay ahead of the curve by investing in new and trending services, frameworks and programming languages.

While we do try to keep open minded, we are not totally agnostic in our technology choices – we do have our favourites! The answer you get might vary depending on which member of the team you
talk to, however we have a tendency towards open source and community driven tools.

We have a passion for Software as a Service (SaaS) projects. Where applications can be built and coded once and then consumed by end users as and when needed. Such solutions can be rolled out to thousands of users at only a fraction of the cost of bespoke development work.

These days, almost more critical than the language or framework used is the platform on which the solution is hosted. We have a huge focus on cloud hosting, Progressive Web Apps (PWAs), machine learning (AI) and serverless implementations.
